* .tar
  * pack:                        tar cvf FileName.tar DirName
  * unpack:                      tar xvf FileName.tar

* .gz
  * compress:                    gzip FileName
  * extract 1:                   gunzip FileName.gz
  * extract 2:                   gzip -d FileName.gz

* .tar.gz
  * pack & compress:             tar zcvf FileName.tar.gz DirName
  * extract & unpack:            tar zxvf FileName.tar.gz

* .bz2
  * compress:                    bzip2 -z FileName
  * extract 1:                   bzip2 -d FileName.bz2
  * extract 2:                   bzip2 FileName.bz2

* .tar.bz2
  * pack & compress:             tar jcvf FileName.tar.bz2 DirName
  * extract & unpack:            tar jxvf FileName.tar.bz2

* .xz
  * compress:                    xz -z FileName
  * extract:                     xz -d FileName.xz

* .tar.xz
  * pack & compress:             tar Jcvf FileName.tar.xz DirName
  * extract & unpack:            tar Jxvf FileName.tar.xz

* .zst
  * compress:                    zst FileName
  * extract:                     zst -d FileName.zst

* .tar.zst
  * pack & compress:             tar -l zstd -cvf FileName.tar.zst DirName
  * extract & unpack:            tar -l zstd -xvf FileName.tar.zst

* .7z
  * compress:                    7z a FileName.7z DirName
  * compress (by password):      7z a FileName.7z DirName -p PASSWORD
  * extract:                     7z x FileName.7z

* .zip
  * compress:                    zip -r FileName.zip DirName
  * extract:                     unzip FileName.zip

* .rar
  * compress:                    rar a FileName.rar DirName
  * extract 1:                   rar e FileName.rar
  * extract 2:                   unrar e FileName.rar
  * extract (specify directory): rar x FileName.rar DirName
